Baked Spaghetti

Home

Description

Baked spaghetti is a comforting pasta dish where cooked spaghetti is mixed with savory meat sauce and cheese, then baked until bubbly and golden. The oven baking gives it a slightly crispy top and a rich, cohesive flavor throughout. It's a hearty, crowd-pleasing meal perfect for family dinners or leftovers.

Ingredients

  • 1 package spaghetti
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 32oz jar of meatless spaghetti sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon seasoned salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/3 cup grated Patmesan cheese
  • 5 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 2 cups small curd cottage cheese
  • 5 cups shredded mozzarella cheese, divided

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  3. Lightly grease baking dish
  4. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil
  5. Cook spaghetti in boiling water, stir occasionally
  6. Drain
  7. Heat a large skillet over medium heat
  8. Cook and stir beef and onion until meat is browned
  9. Drain excell oil from the pan
  10. Stir in spaghetti sauce and seasoned salt
  11. Whisk eggs, Parmesan, and butter in a large bowl
  12. Add spaghetti to the bowl and toss to coat
  13. Place 1/2 of spaghetti mixture in baking dish
  14. Top with 1/2 of the cottage cheese, 2 cups mozzarella, and 1/2 of the meat sauce
  15. Repeat layers
  16. Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 40 minutes
  17. Remove the foil and sprinkle with remaining 1 cup of mozzarella
  18. Bake for another 20 minutes
  19. Serve and enjoy!
