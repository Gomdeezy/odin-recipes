Banana Pancakes

Home

Description

Banana pancakes are a sweet and fluffy breakfast dish made by mixing mashed ripe bananas into a traditional pancake batter. The bananas add natural sweetness and moisture, making them soft and flavorful without needing much added sugar. Often served with syrup, butter, or fresh fruit, they're a cozy and nutritious twist on a classic morning favorite.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • 1 egg
  • Whole milk
  • Vegetable oil
  • Bananas

Steps

  1. Combine dry ingredients in one bowl
  2. Combine wet ingredients in another bowl
  3. Stir both bowls
  4. Add dry ingredients to wet ingredients
  5. Pour batter into lightly oiled pan over medium heat
  6. Cook for a few minutes and then flip
  7. Cook for another few minutes. Each side should be golden brown!
  8. Serve immediately! Make sure you top them with your favorite syrup and goodies!
